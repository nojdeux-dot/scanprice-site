Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

ScanPrice is a barcode scanning app that helps you find the best price for any product. This policy explains what data we handle and how.

Data We Collect

ScanPrice does not require an account and does not collect any personal information.

The only data stored is your local scan history, which is saved on your device only using your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time from within the app.

Camera Access

ScanPrice requests access to your device's camera solely to scan product barcodes. Camera data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted, stored, or shared.

Price Lookup Requests

When you scan a barcode, ScanPrice sends a price lookup request to our backend server. This request includes:

  • The barcode number
  • A short product keyword derived from the barcode

No personal information, device identifiers, or location data is included in these requests. Our server uses this information only to query third-party pricing services and return results to your device. We do not log or store these requests.

Third-Party Services

ScanPrice queries the following third-party services to retrieve product and pricing information:

  • eBay — active listing prices via the eBay Browse API (eBay Privacy Policy)
  • Amazon — affiliate search links via the Amazon Associates programme (Amazon Privacy Policy)
  • CeX / WeBuy — buy and sell prices (CeX Privacy Policy)
  • UPCitemdb — product name lookup from barcode (UPCitemdb Privacy Policy)

These services may have their own data practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Affiliate Disclosure

ScanPrice participates in affiliate programmes including the Amazon Associates Programme and the eBay Partner Network. When you tap a link to Amazon or eBay, it may be an affiliate link. If you make a qualifying purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This helps support the development of the app.

Clicking affiliate links may result in a cookie being set by Amazon or eBay on your device, which they use to track qualifying purchases. This is governed by their respective privacy policies. We do not receive any personal information about you as a result of these cookies.

Analytics & Advertising

ScanPrice does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Children's Privacy

ScanPrice is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
