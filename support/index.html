Frequently Asked Questions

Why are prices not showing?

Prices are fetched live from eBay and CeX. If results aren't showing, check that you have an active internet connection. Some items may not have listings available on all platforms.

Why is the barcode not scanning?

Make sure you've granted camera permission to ScanPrice. Hold the camera steady and ensure the barcode is well-lit and fully visible within the frame. Most standard EAN-13 and UPC barcodes are supported.

Can I delete my scan history?

Yes — go to the History tab, tap the bin icon to delete individual items, or use the Clear All option to remove your entire history. All history is stored locally on your device only.

Why does the CeX section show a button instead of prices?

CeX pricing is retrieved via a live connection to their website. Occasionally this connection may be unavailable — tapping the button will take you directly to the CeX search results for that item.

The product name looks wrong after scanning

Product names are looked up from barcode databases. Less common or regional products may return incomplete information. The search results may still be accurate even if the product name shown isn't perfect.
